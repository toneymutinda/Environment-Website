Company Profile



Who Are We?

At TreeStop Agency, our work speaks for itself through the numerous awards and recognitions that we have received in East and Central Africa. Ours is a vocation that not only entails conserving the environment, but also ensuring that the tree count in the regions that we visit scales up.

Boasting of highly trained professionals, we strive to ensure that our ethos of conserve, preserve and nurture are adhered to. In 2015, we received an award for the Best Nature Company in East and Central Africa. This was just the start, as we have received 9 more awards since then. A big thank you to you all!
